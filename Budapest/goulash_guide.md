## Stand25 Bistro ⭐ Best Overall

**Address:** [Attila út 10, 1013 Budapest (Castle District, Buda)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/LaRXbPb8rZPc9ant7)  
**Price:** ~6,500 HUF (~€17)  
**Hours:** Tue–Sat 12:00–22:00

Michelin Bib Gourmand. Paprika-hued goulash with tender beef, refined technique. Seasonal menu beyond goulash.

**Tips:** Book in advance — essential. 15% service charge auto-added. Counter seats have kitchen views.  
**Phone:** +36 30 785 9139

## Gettó Gulyás

**Address:** [Wesselényi u. 18, 1077 Budapest (Jewish Quarter)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/RCVGer6vymprgw8j9)  
**Price:** 4,000–4,400 HUF (~€10–11)  
**Hours:** Daily 12:00–23:00

Rustic Jewish Quarter staple. Traditional goulash + pörkölt (thick stew). Vintage Zsolnay plates, bone marrow starters.

**Tips:** Reserve weekends. Atmosphere > consistency.  
**Phone:** +36 20 376 4480

---

## Menza Étterem

**Address:** [Liszt Ferenc tér 2, 1061 Budapest](https://maps.app.goo.gl/WXCKpzZuNurRa2CQ7)  
**Price:** 3,300 HUF with csipetke (~€8)  
**Hours:** Daily 11:00–23:00

Two-decade staple on a lively square. Rich, spicy goulash, never disappoints. Terrace for people-watching.

**Tips:** Reserve weekends. 12% service charge standard. Golden hour best for terrace.  
**Phone:** +36 1 413 1482

## Pozsonyi Kisvendéglő ⭐ Best Budget

**Address:** [Radnóti Miklós u. 38, 1137 Budapest (Újlipótváros)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/qoGs6tVYUgRpHP6JA)  
**Price:** Under 2,400 HUF (~€6)  
**Hours:** Mon–Fri 11:30–22:00 | Sat 12:00–22:00

Grandmother-kitchen authenticity. Massive portions, 22+ years consistency. "Tastes like Hungarian grandma's kitchen."

**Tips:** CASH ONLY. Entrance on Radnóti Miklós utca (not Pozsonyi út). Service abrupt but efficient. Dogs welcome.  
**Phone:** +36 1 787-4877

## KIOSK Budapest

**Address:** [Március 15. tér 4, 1056 Budapest (Danube riverfront)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/WCpMmwK6Wvek2Tc89)  
**Price:** 5,200–6,000 HUF (~€14–16)  
**Hours:** Daily 12:00–23:00 (kitchen break 16:30–17:30)

Riverside Danube views. Rich, filling goulash with generous high-quality meat. Modern spin, in-house bakery.

**Tips:** Book riverside tables for sunset. Kitchen break 16:30–17:30 — plan around it.  
**Phone:** +36 70 585 5727

## Frici Papa

**Address:** [Király u. 55, 1077 Budapest](https://maps.app.goo.gl/fswzMBYDXwnScDeZ6)  
**Price:** 2,400–3,000 HUF (~€6–8)  
**Hours:** Mon–Fri & Sun 11:00–23:00 | **Closed Saturday**

Casual local cafeteria. Rich, spicy goulash, enormous portions. No-frills, walk-ins only.

**Tips:** Walk-ins only (no reservations). Bread auto-charged even if untouched. Staff famously abrupt — normal, don't take personally.  
**Phone:** +36 1 351 0197

---

## Bestia ⭐ Spiciest

**Address:** [Szent István tér 9-11, 1051 Budapest (near St. Stephen's Basilica)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/e37JEymHGFcWRwG47)  
**Price:** ~3,000 HUF (~€8)  
**Hours:** Daily 12:00–00:00

American-style meat pub. Fiery goulash served in small pot with sourdough. Also famous for Josper charcoal BBQ ribs.

**Tips:** Spiciest goulash in Budapest — bring water. Use sourdough to cool between spoonfuls. Reserve ahead.  
**Phone:** +36 1 269 3570

---

## Gundel Étterem (Heritage Pick)

**Address:** [Gundel Károly út 4, 1146 Budapest (City Park)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/btygrKvP9PSaKnoN9)  
**Price:** ~3,600 HUF (~€9)  
**Hours:** Daily 11:30–22:00

Budapest's most historic fine-dining restaurant. Elegant goulash from rich beef cheek in luxurious surroundings.

**Tips:** Convenient if visiting City Park / Heroes' Square. Priced accordingly for the heritage experience.  
**Phone:** +36 30 603 2480
