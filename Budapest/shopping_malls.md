# Budapest Shopping Malls

Source: https://monkeywalker.com/shopping-mall-in-budapest

---

## WestEnd Shopping Center

**Address:** Váci út 1-3, 1062 Budapest  
**Hours:** Daily 8:00 AM – 11:00 PM

City-center mall above Nyugati train station. 3 floors + 1 basement. Direct metro/tram access below.

**Highlights:** Cinema, food court, rooftop garden, Adidas, Nike, H&M, Pandora, LUSH  
**Transit:** M3 (Nyugati pályaudvar), Trams 4/6  
**Tips:** Luggage storage €1/hr. Free WiFi. Adjacent to Hilton Budapest City.

---

## Arena Mall

**Address:** Kerepesi út 9, 1087 Budapest  
**Hours:** Mon–Sat 10:00 AM – 9:00 PM | Sun 10:00 AM – 7:00 PM

Near Keleti train station. Popular with travelers.

**Highlights:** Cinema, Calvin Klein, Pull & Bear, Michael Kors, TESCO supermarket, Sport Direct  
**Transit:** M2/M4 (Keleti pályaudvar)  
**Tips:** Free WiFi. Nursing room available (1000 HUF key deposit).

---

## Allee Mall

**Address:** Október huszonharmadika u. 8-10, 1117 Budapest  
**Hours:** Mon–Sat 10:00 AM – 9:00 PM | Sun 10:00 AM – 7:00 PM

Buda side, quieter than central malls. Good for relaxed shopping.

**Highlights:** Zara, H&M, Office Shoes. Big discounts during holiday seasons.  
**Transit:** Trams 4, 6, 17, 19, 41, 49, 61; M4 (Újbuda-központ)  
**Tips:** Free WiFi. Weekdays especially uncrowded.

---

## ÁRKÁD Budapest

**Address:** Örs vezér tere 25/A, 1106 Budapest  
**Hours:** Mon–Sat 10:00 AM – 9:00 PM | Sun 10:00 AM – 7:00 PM

Connected via underground passage to metro. IKEA adjacent.

**Highlights:** Major brand retailers, extensive food court, home goods  
**Transit:** M2 (Örs vezér tere), Trams 3, 62, 62A  
**Tips:** Free WiFi. Gets busy during evening rush hours.

---

## The Whale (Bálna) Cultural Center

**Address:** Fővám tér 11-12, 1093 Budapest  
**Hours:** Daily 10:00 AM – 10:00 PM

Whale-shaped glass structure on the Danube riverbank. Shopping + contemporary art gallery.

**Highlights:** Outdoor riverside seating, cultural exhibitions, summer sunset views  
**Transit:** M4 (Fővám tér), Trams 2, 2M  
**Tips:** Free WiFi. Best in the evening for river ambiance. Walking distance to Central Market Hall.

---

## Etele Plaza

**Address:** Etele út 68, 1115 Budapest  
**Hours:** Check https://eteleplaza.hu/en/opening-hours/

Opened 2021 in southern Budapest. 180+ shops across 3 floors with rooftop terrace.

**Highlights:** Cinema, family dining zones, interactive games, multiple restaurant brands  
**Transit:** M4 (Kelenföld vasútállomás), Tram 1, Kelenföld train station  
**Tips:** Free WiFi. ~30 min from city center.
